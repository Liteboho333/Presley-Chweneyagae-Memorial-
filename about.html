About Presley

Presley Chweneyagae was born on 19 October 1984 in Mafikeng, South Africa. He was an actor and producer, known for Tsotsi (2005), The River (2018) and Mandela: Long Walk to Freedom (2013). He died on 27 May 2025 in Johannesburg, South Africa.

Chweneyagae made his TV acting debut when he was cast as The Poet in the 2006 mini-series “When We Were Black”. He gained global recognition for his role in the critically acclaimed film “Tsotsi”, which won an Academy Award (Oscar) for Best Foreign Language Film in 2006.

Died: May 27, 2025 (age 40 years), Johannesburg, South Africa His family said he had died from natural causes after experiencing breathing problems. Family spokesperson Mzwakhe Sigudla said that paramedics had attended to him, but he "couldn't make it".
